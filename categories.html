Coolest Projects Awards

The Coolest Projects awards are our most important event and it has importance for the computing world as well. The main purpose of this event is to support and inspire a generation of innovators, creators and entrepreneurs between 7 and 17 years old. Therefore, once a year, children and teenagers have the opportunity to show, in a real competition and exhibition, the results of their hard work in CoderDojo tutorials and, even more, to receive a prize for that.

Every year Coolest Projects awards has became bigger and bigger. This year’s event featured more than 500 entries from young people across all over the world and attracted thousands of spectators to see and admire this year's projects.
